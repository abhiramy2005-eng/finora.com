FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Is my financial data secure with Finora?

Absolutely. We use 256-bit encryption, are SOC 2 Type II certified, and connect to your banks through read-only links. We never store your banking credentials and cannot move money.

How does the AI generate insights?

Finora analyzes your historical transactions, revenue, and spending patterns to model cash flow and surface recommendations. All analysis is specific to your business—no generic advice.

Which banks and tools can I connect?

We support over 12,000 banks worldwide plus popular tools like Stripe, PayPal, QuickBooks, and Xero. New integrations are added every month.

Can my whole team use Finora?

Yes. Invite teammates, assign roles, and share dashboards. The Pro plan includes up to 10 members, and Enterprise supports unlimited seats with advanced permissions.

Do I need a credit card to start?

No. The Starter plan is free forever, and the 14-day Pro trial requires no credit card. Upgrade only when you're ready.

Can I cancel or change plans anytime?

Of course. Upgrade, downgrade, or cancel from your dashboard at any time—no contracts, no cancellation fees.
